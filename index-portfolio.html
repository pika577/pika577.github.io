About

Who I Am

I'm a high school developer based in Ottawa with a genuine interest in the intersection of technology and economics. My passion centres on building software that makes complex data interpretable — particularly in financial and policy contexts where rigorous analysis underpins real decisions.

I've explored this through an AI research internship at Appli AI, market analysis work at a financial coaching firm, and an ongoing personal project that visualizes Ontario's macroeconomic indicators using live Statistics Canada data. I'm drawn to problems where engineering and analytical thinking work together.

Outside of technical work, I've spent five years in a leadership role within a cadet squadron and founded my school's business club — experiences that have reinforced my ability to take initiative, communicate clearly, and follow through under pressure.
